MY EDUCATION JOURNEY

Kindergarten

I started to enter school at the age of 4 years old at Tadika Ummi. When I turned 5 years old I transferred to another kindergarten near my house at Tadika Tambak Bunga

Primary School

My primary school loacated at Sekolah Kebangsaan Taman Rakyat from 7 Years Old until 12 Years Old. It holds a lot of unforgettable memories that i still remember vividly.

Secondary School

My secondary school loacated at Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Sultanah Bahiyah, Alor Setar, Kedah. It is an all girls school. SMK Sultanah Bahiyah once hold a recognition as an International Baccalaureate World School for 5 Years.

Diploma

I am a part 5 student of Information Management in UITM Kedah. UiTM Kedah Branch is one of the branches of Universiti Teknologi MARA established in Kedah, Malaysia. It is administered by a Rector and is responsible for a UiTM campus in Kedah which is Sungai Petani.
